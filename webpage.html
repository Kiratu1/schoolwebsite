WHO WE ARE

At Harmony Springs Academy, we blend the innovative Montessori approach with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) to offer a unique and holistic educational experience. Our school is dedicated to fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment where each child can explore their passions, develop essential skills, and achieve their full potential. With a commitment to academic excellence, personalized learning, and a wide range of extracurricular activities, we prepare students to thrive both in their studies and in life. Join us in cultivating a vibrant community of learners who are inspired to grow, lead, and succeed.
